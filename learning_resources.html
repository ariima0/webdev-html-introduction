Learning Resources at Cantor College

At Cantor College, we are committed to supporting your learning journey with a variety of resources tailored to help you succeed. From our extensive library services to cutting-edge online tools, you’ll have everything you need to thrive academically.

Library Services

Our on-campus library is equipped with a wide selection of books, journals, and periodicals. Students can also access digital resources such as e-books, academic databases, and research articles through the library portal. Study spaces, quiet zones, and group discussion areas are available to suit your needs.

Online Learning Tools

We provide access to an array of online platforms, including:

  • Moodle: Your one-stop platform for coursework, assignments, and class materials.
  • Microsoft Teams: Collaborate with peers and faculty for virtual learning sessions and group projects.
  • Library Portal: Access digital resources like e-books and academic journals from anywhere.

Workshops and Training

Enhance your skills by attending workshops on research methods, time management, academic writing, and more. Check the events calendar to see upcoming training sessions and reserve your spot!

IT Support

Our IT support team is available to assist you with any technical issues related to accessing learning tools, setting up accounts, or troubleshooting software problems.

Study Spaces

Take advantage of our dedicated study zones, equipped with modern furniture, high-speed internet, and charging stations. Whether you need a quiet space or a collaborative environment, we’ve got you covered.
